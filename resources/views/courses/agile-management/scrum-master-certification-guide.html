Overview

Certifications are the industry-recognized way to grow your skills and take your career to new heights. Having a Scrum Master certification shows that your Scrum Master skills are up to date, you’re willing to learn new things and are committed to professional excellence. It also helps you market yourself better and command higher salaries than your non-certified peers.

However, with several organisations now offering their own learning path to ultimately achieve certification, how do you decide which is right for you, your team, and your organisation? In this Scrum Master - Getting Started Guide, we introduce the main certification-issuing organizations (accreditation bodies) along with the certifications they issue.

Before we dive into the specifics of Scrum Master certifications, let’s first take you through the basics of what is Agile and Scrum and the role of the Scrum Master.

What is Agile?

Agile is a term used to describe an iterative approach to the development of products and solutions. Centred on iterative development, Agile emphasizes incremental delivery, team collaboration, continual planning, and continual learning, instead of delivering all of it at once towards the end.

.. Read more

With a manifesto and a set of values and principles, Agile is a mindset - a way of thinking and acting - rather than a rigid set of processes and practices. This dynamic approach has proven to be highly beneficial for businesses and big names across the industry have embraced it.

What is Scrum?

Scrum is the most popular agile framework used to deliver working products or solutions in increments of 30 days or less called "sprints”. It is a lightweight framework that helps people, teams and organizations address complex adaptive problems and deliver the highest value.

.. Read more

Leveraging the collective intelligence of the people using it, rather than provide them with detailed instructions, Scrum exhibits a powerful set of values, principles, and practices that enable businesses to achieve:

  • Increased ability to manage changing priorities
  • Better visibility into projects
  • More alignment between business and IT
  • Faster time to market
  • Increased team collaboration
  • Increased customer satisfaction
  • Maximum ROI on development

The Scrum Master

A scrum master is a facilitator, coach, mentor, and servant leader that guides the development team through executing Scrum correctly. The Scrum Master heads the Scrum events and removes impediments, empowering the development team to self-organize, create, innovate, and make decisions for themselves as a unit.

Bagging the spotlight at the sixth most emerging job in product development and among the top 10 most promising careers of the future, the role of the Scrum Master is much sought after across industries. With the average salary for certified scrum masters between $100,000 and $130,000, it’s a great time for Scrum Masters be in the market.

Steps to get certified

How do you apply for the certification? Do you have the option to take the exam, both physically and online? Can you download the certificate immediately or would you need to wait to receive this by email? Will you receive a physical copy? Oftentimes, it’s these types of specifics that are unclear and create stumbling blocks in your path to your Scrum Master certification. Find out the exact set of steps to get the Scrum Master certification of your choice.

Certified ScrumMaster® (CSM®)

Get familiar with the 12 principles and 4 values stated in the Agile Manifesto through our popular in-class activity- “Draw and demonstrate”. Herein, you will be asked to form groups and illustrate all 12 principles pictorially.

Step 1: Get trained

Get trained through a Registered Education Provider (REP) of Scrum Alliance. Enrol for an upcoming Certified ScrumMaster® course and attend the 2-day training (of at least 14 hours).

Step 2: Take the exam

Log in to Scrum Alliance, complete your profile, answer 'yes' or 'no' to the question on Marketing Opt-in, and accept the License Agreement. Then take the one-hour online CSM test and get a minimum score of 74%.

Step 3: Become a CSM!

On passing the CSM test, download the CSM® certificate and become a leader in your Scrum team!

Professional Scrum Master™ (PSM™)

Step 1: Get trained

Get trained through a Professional Training Network (PTN) member of Scrum.org. Enrol for an upcoming Professional Scrum Master™ I course

Step 2: Take the exam

Click on the Scrum.org exam link (you will receive the link with Assessment key after the course), take the online PSM I exam and get a minimum score of 85% in the 60-min exam.

Step 3: Become a PSM!

Download the PSM™ certificate. You're now a Scrum.org certified Professional Scrum Master!

Disciplined Agile Scrum Master (DASM)

Step 1: Get trained

Get trained through an Authorized Training Partner of PMI®. Enrol for an upcoming DASM course and attend the 2-day training (of at least 14 hours).

Step 2: Take the exam

Schedule your certification exam at a Pearson VUE test center or via the online proctored testing solution, using the eligibility code you receive from PMI after completing the training. Take the closed-book multi-choice 90-min exam and get at least 70 percent (35 out of 50 questions right).

Step 3: Become a DASM!

On clearing the exam, you get designated as a Disciplined Agile Scrum Master.

Certified SAFe® 5 Scrum Master (SSM)

Step 1: Get trained

Get trained by an Authorized Training Partner of Scaled Agile, Inc.® (Scaled Agile). Enrol for an upcoming SSM course and attend the 2-day training (of at least 14 hours), delivered by a SAFe® Program Consultant (SPC) who’s also an expert practitioner.

Step 2: Take the exam

Log in using credentials you will receive directly from Scaled Agile on completion of the two-day training, take the 90-minute, 45-MCQ online closed-book exam. Clear the exam and get 34 out of 45 questions right to pass the SAFe® 5 exam and 33 out of 45 questions right to pass the SAFe® 4 exam.

Step 3: Become an SSM!

On passing the exam, you become a Certified SAFe® 5 Scrum Master. You will receive your SAFe® 5 Scrum Master PDF Certificate and Digital Badge within 5-7 working days.

Scrum Master by Scrum Inc.™

Step 1: Get trained

To get trained through KnowledgeHut, enrol for an upcoming Scrum Master by Scrum Inc. course and attend the 2-day training, delivered by an experienced expert practitioner.

Step 2: Take the exam

Log in using credentials you will receive directly from Scrum Inc. on completion of the two-day training. You have 30 days to take the exam after completing the training. Get a minimum of 23 questions right out of 30 to pass the exam. There is no time limit once the exam is started.

 

Take the exam and clear it.

Step 3: Become an SISM!

On passing the exam, you can download your certificate and become a Scrum Master by Scrum Inc.!
